About NoneCloud

Powering Your Gameplay with Performance, Reliability, and Simplicity.

Our Commitment to Gamers

NoneCloud is a premium provider of game hosting nodes designed to deliver reliable, high-performance infrastructure for gamers and server owners worldwide.

At NoneCloud, we understand the importance of low latency and uptime when it comes to gaming. That’s why all our nodes are built on cutting-edge hardware and connected to high-speed networks, ensuring smooth gameplay and minimal lag. Whether you’re hosting popular games like Minecraft, CS:GO, Rust, or any multiplayer title, NoneCloud provides the foundation you need to offer your players the best experience possible.

Why Choose NoneCloud?

We combine powerful technology with a passion for gaming to deliver an unparalleled hosting experience.

Powered by Pterodactyl

Our platform leverages the power of Pterodactyl, a modern and open-source panel. This gives you a secure, intuitive dashboard to create, customize, and control your servers with ease.

Advanced Security

Security is a top priority. Our nodes are protected with advanced DDoS mitigation and firewall configurations to keep your servers safe from attacks, and we follow strict privacy standards.

Passionate Community & Support

NoneCloud is more than just infrastructure — we’re a community of gamers and hosts. Our dedicated support team is available to help you anytime via Discord or our ticket system.

Simplified Administration

From automated backups to one-click installs and real-time resource monitoring, we simplify server administration so you can focus on your community and gameplay.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our company and services.

What is the mission of NoneCloud?

Our mission is to provide reliable, high-performance, and affordable game server infrastructure for gamers and communities worldwide. We aim to simplify server management so you can focus on what matters most: playing and building your community.

What technology powers the control panel?

We use the powerful and user-friendly Pterodactyl Panel. It's an open-source, modern management panel trusted by thousands, offering a secure and intuitive dashboard for complete control over your game servers.

How do you ensure server performance and low latency?

All our nodes are built on cutting-edge hardware, including high-frequency CPUs and NVMe SSDs. We connect them to premium, high-speed networks to ensure the lowest possible latency and a smooth, lag-free gameplay experience.

Who is the founder of NoneCloud?

NoneCloud was founded by Muhammad Wafiq, a passionate gamer and hosting expert. His vision is to build a service that offers the quality, performance, and support that he would want as a server owner himself.

NoneCloud offers high-performance, low-latency game server hosting at nonecloud.top with DDoS protection and a user-friendly Pterodactyl panel.
