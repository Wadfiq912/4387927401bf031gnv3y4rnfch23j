Pricing Questions

Answers to common questions about our plans and billing.

What happens if my server needs more resources?

You can upgrade your plan at any time through our client area with just a few clicks. The system is fully automated, and you'll only pay the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a money-back guarantee within 7 days of your initial purchase. To request a refund, please contact our support team via Discord or email with valid proof of transaction. All requests are reviewed within 72 hours. Please note that refunds are not available for policy violations, service misuse, or user-side errors.

Are there any hidden fees or contracts?

Absolutely not. The price you see is the price you pay. We believe in transparent pricing. All our plans are billed monthly without any long-term contracts, so you can cancel anytime.

Which server location should I choose?

For the lowest ping and best gameplay experience, we recommend choosing the server location geographically closest to you and your players. We currently offer servers in Malaysia, Singapore, and Indonesia.
