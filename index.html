Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to set up my game server?

All our game servers are set up instantly and automatically after payment is confirmed. You will receive an email with all your login details within a few minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), PayPal, and various local payment gateways for your convenience.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, absolutely! You can easily upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time through our client area. The system will automatically calculate the price difference.

Is DDoS protection included with all plans?

Yes, every single game server we host comes with our standard, always-on DDoS protection. Our advanced plans include an even more robust level of protection for high-traffic servers.
